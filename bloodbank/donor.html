Why donate blood ?

Curbs the risk of heart attack


  • Excessive iron can be one of the reasons for heart attack. Doctors indicate that the risk is more in men than women

  • Higher amounts of iron can also lead to increased cholesterol levels.
  • Thus, blood donation is one way to maintain healthy iron levels in the bloodstream and minimize the risk of high cholesterol.

    • Promotes weight loss

  • The human body spends energy to regenerate the donated blood.
  • Approximately, 650 calories of fat burn happen in this process.
  • Thus, blood donation boosts metabolism and helps fat burn, helping in overall weight management.

    • Strengthens immunity

    • After the blood donation, the spleen revitalizes itself to produce new blood cells.
    • Even under normal condition, the human body changes its blood once every three months.
    • Thus, it is a misnomer that donating blood deprives you of blood.
    • In contrast, after a blood donation, your body gets into an activation mode faster, leading to strengthened immunity

    Reduces stress and related risks

    • Overweight and stress are directly related.
    • Thus, blood donation helps to kick away stress and its related risks like early aging and wrinkles.
    • The feel-good hormones that are released after the blood donation also benefit your emotional health.

    Free health check-up

    • Doctors test the overall health condition and history before a person can donate blood.
    • Thus, every blood donor undergoes a free health check-up before the donation.
    • Besides, the blood sample is tested for HIV, West Nile Virus, hepatitis B and C, and Syphilis.
    • Thus, at a blood camp, a donor gets a free health and blood check-up.

    Turn into a life saviour

    • Blood cannot be stored for a long time.
    • There is always a constant demand for safe blood.
    • Thus, donating blood is a way to turn yourself into a life saviour.
    • The satisfaction of saving a life (read, 3 lives) is priceless!
